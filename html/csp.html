Camaraderie, effficiency, and Canadian heritage is the driving force of the Canadian Ski Patrol. The history and everyday tools of the CSP gave way for the visual identity. This comes to life in the form of a graphic patch motif used as a framing tool, the CSP yurt intergrated into the logomark, and typography and colours reminiscent of 2000s Canadian culture.
